TOP 10 MOBILE GAMES OF 2023


                                 1.POKEMON GO

                                                               

-> Pokémon GO is a mobile augmented reality (AR) game developed and published by Niantic in collaboration with Nintendo and The Pokémon Company. The game was released in July 2016 for iOS and Android devices. Pokémon GO is part of the Pokémon franchise, which originated with the Pokémon video games and includes various forms of media. In Pokémon GO, players use their mobile devices' GPS and camera to explore the real world and catch virtual Pokémon that appear on the screen. The game encourages outdoor exploration and social interaction as players can encounter Pokémon in different locations, and various landmarks and real-world points of interest serve as PokéStops and Gyms in the game.
The primary objective of Pokémon GO is to catch Pokémon, train them, and battle other players at Gyms. The game uses augmented reality technology to overlay Pokémon onto the real-world environment, making it seem as though the creatures are present in the player's surroundings. Since its launch, Pokémon GO has received regular updates, introducing new features, generations of Pokémon, and special events. The game's success is attributed to its innovative use of augmented reality, the nostalgic appeal of Pokémon, and the community aspect, as players often collaborate during special events or while participating in raids.

                                 2.Call of Duty:Mobile

                                                               

-> Call of Duty Mobile is a free-to-play first-person shooter game developed by TiMi Studios and published by Activision and Garena. It was released in October 2019 for iOS and Android devices. The game brings the popular Call of Duty franchise to mobile platforms, offering a mobile-optimized experience with various game modes and features. Call of Duty Mobile features a combination of multiplayer modes, including Team Deathmatch, Domination, and Battle Royale. The game also includes iconic maps from previous Call of Duty titles, and players can choose from a variety of classic Call of Duty characters, weapons, and equipment.
The Battle Royale mode in Call of Duty Mobile is a significant aspect of the game, allowing players to engage in a large-scale, last-person-standing battle on a map that gradually shrinks. Players can explore the map, pick up weapons and equipment, and compete against others to be the sole survivor. The game has received positive reviews for its graphics, controls, and the overall experience it brings to mobile devices. Call of Duty Mobile is regularly updated with new content, including maps, game modes, and events, keeping the gameplay fresh and engaging for players.

                                 3.Among Us

                                                               

->Among Us is a social deception game which can be downloaded for free on Android and iOs, or to purchase for use on a PC. It can be played with a group of between 4 and 10 players either
online by joining a game with strangers, or players can host or join games with friends.The premise of the game is simple – players are given the role of either a crewmate or an impostor. The crewmates must complete a list of tasks on a spaceship to get it ready for departure. Some tasks such as ‘Clean O2 Filter’ are relatively quick to complete, while others such as ‘Fix Weather Node’ require multiple steps. However, the game has at least one impostor who is trying to sabotage the ship and kill all the crew. As the game continues the impostor undermines and starts to kill members of the crew. The game is paused when a body is discovered or to call an emergency meeting to discuss who the impostor might be and why. Players will have been paying attention to the behaviour of others in the game to try and figure out who the impostor on the ship is. During the discussion players will vote for who they think was acting suspiciously enough to be the impostor. If a player gets enough votes they will be ejected from the game, and whether they were a crewmate, or an impostor will be revealed.
To win the game the crewmates must complete all the tasks or discover the impostor. The impostor will win if they can avoid detection and kill off the whole crew.

-> Free Fire Max is an enhanced version of Garena Free Fire, a popular battle royale mobile game. Free Fire Max is designed to provide players with an upgraded and more immersive gaming experience compared to the standard Free Fire game.
Free Fire Max retains the core elements of Free Fire, such as the battle royale gameplay where 50 players compete to be the last person or team standing on a shrinking map. However, Free Fire Max features improved graphics, higher resolution textures, and enhanced visual effects, delivering a more visually appealing and detailed gaming experience. The game is optimized for high-end devices with better hardware capabilities, allowing players to enjoy smoother graphics and improved performance. Free Fire Max also supports features like higher frame rates, making it a choice for players who want a more graphically intensive and immersive battle royale experience.

                                 5.Genshin Impact

                                                               

-> Genshin Impact is an action role-playing game developed and published by the Chinese gaming company miHoYo. The game was released in September 2020 for various platforms, including Microsoft Windows, PlayStation 4, iOS, and Android. It's a free-to-play game with optional in-game purchases. Genshin Impact is set in the fantasy world of Teyvat, which is home to seven distinct nations, each based on different real-world cultures and elements. Players take on the role of the "Traveler," a character who has arrived in Teyvat from another world and is on a quest to find their lost sibling.
One of the standout features of Genshin Impact is its open-world exploration, where players can travel across a vast and visually stunning landscape, solving puzzles, completing quests, and engaging in real-time combat. The game incorporates an elemental system, with characters possessing different elemental affinities that can be combined for various effects during battles and exploration. Genshin Impact gained significant attention for its beautiful art style, expansive open world, and the ability to switch between characters with different elemental abilities. Additionally, it has a gacha system for acquiring new characters and weapons.

                                 6.Subway Surfers

                                                               

-> Subway Surfers is a popular endless runner mobile game developed by Kiloo and SYBO Games. The game was released in May 2012 and is available on various platforms, including iOS, Android, and Windows Phone. In Subway Surfers, players take on the role of graffiti artists who, upon being caught in the act of "tagging" a subway station, run through the subway tracks to escape from the inspector and his dog. The gameplay involves the characters running through the subway, dodging oncoming trains and obstacles, and collecting coins and power-ups along the way. The game is known for its vibrant and colorful graphics, simple controls, and constant updates that introduce new locations, characters, and challenges. The goal is to run as far as possible, avoiding obstacles and collecting coins to unlock new characters and power-ups. Subway Surfers has maintained its popularity over the years, thanks to its engaging gameplay and regular updates.

                                 7.Asphalt 9

                                                               

-> Asphalt 9: Legends is a popular racing video game developed by Gameloft. It is the ninth installment in the Asphalt series and was released in July 2018 for various platforms, including iOS, Android, Windows, and Nintendo Switch. The game is known for its high-quality graphics, realistic gameplay, and a wide range of licensed cars. Asphalt 9 features a variety of game modes, including a career mode, multiplayer races, and special events. Players can race against AI opponents or compete with other players in real-time multiplayer races. The game includes a vast selection of over 50 high-performance cars from manufacturers like Ferrari, Lamborghini, Porsche, and more.
One of the notable aspects of Asphalt 9 is its use of a touch drive control system, allowing players to swipe on the screen to control their cars. This simplified control scheme aims to make the game accessible to a wide audience while maintaining an immersive racing experience. Asphalt 9 has received positive reviews for its stunning visuals, engaging gameplay, and the variety of cars and tracks available. It has continued to receive updates and new content since its initial release, keeping the gaming experience fresh for players.

-> Roblox is an app that allows users to play a wide variety of games, create games, and chat with others online. It combines gaming, social media, and social commerce. Billing itself as the “ultimate virtual universe,” Roblox experiences are places where users can socialize, build their own spaces, and even earn and spend virtual money. Games on Roblox are officially called “experiences” which fall into a variety of genres. Users can dabble in games tagged as roleplay, adventure, fighting, obby (obstacle courses), tycoon, simulator, and more.
Many of the most popular games on the app, including Adopt Me! And Brookhaven RP, fall into the roleplay category. These are less games and more virtual hangouts. Millennials, think of them like Gen Z’s version of Club Penguin. Other categories focus more on agility, strategy, or skill.
Though the platform itself is free, users can make purchases within each experience. A portion of the sales (about 28 cents per dollar spent) goes back to the game’s creator. That means brands and makers of all ages can earn money if the games they build become popular. It really takes user-generated content to a whole new level.
